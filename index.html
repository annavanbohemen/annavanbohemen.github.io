All about me

I did some basic HTML/CSS and worked on the company Sharepoint. But I wanted to learn real programming. Thanks to the Codaisseur Academy I learned to program in only ten weeks. In those ten weeks I learned a lot! Front-end and Back-end: Javascript, HTML5, CSS, React, Redux, Node, Typescript, etcetera. Now I want to take my newly developed skills and use them in the real world.
